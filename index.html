00 / About 01

Data scientist and researcher at the University of Tartu with an interdisciplinary background spanning chemistry, microfluidics, and machine learning. Currently a PhD student working on uncertainty quantification and trustworthy AI.

Heili's academic journey began in the natural sciences: a BSc in Environmental Engineering and an MSc in Gene Technology from the University of Tartu, before transitioning into data science. She holds an MSc in Andmeteadus (Data Science) and has published on printed porous materials for microfluidic applications.

Outside research, she is passionate about the arts, playing the violin, hiking, running, orienteering, and bouldering.
